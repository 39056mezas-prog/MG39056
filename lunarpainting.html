Our
5-Step
Process.

Every project follows the same disciplined method.

01
Preparation

Step 01

Getting Ready

We move furniture, cover everything with plastic sheeting, and protect floors with drop cloths before a single brush touches paint.

02
Surface Prep

Step 02

Proper Prep

Holes filled, cracks sealed, stains primed, surfaces sanded smooth. The foundation of a lasting finish is always in the preparation.

03
Painting

Step 03

The Painting

Proper application techniques using premium Sherwin Williams products deliver a uniform, lasting finish — every time.

04
Cleanup

Step 04

Cleaning Up

We restore everything to its original place, vacuum and sweep, and remove all materials. Your home is left cleaner than we found it.

05
Inspection

Step 05

Final Walk

We walk through the completed work together. Any touch-up needed gets handled immediately — we don't leave until you're satisfied.
