Case Study · 2023

Beattus Cattus Skate Shop

From Zero to Festival — Building a Brand from the Ground Up

Marketing Director

The Situation

A new skateshop opened at Playa Hermosa in Ensenada. The owner had vision and a community behind him, but no brand identity, no digital presence, and a major event on the horizon — the inaugural SK8 Fest Ensenada, tied to the grand opening of one of the best skateparks in Baja California and Baja California Sur.

What We Built

  • → Logo design & full brand identity
  • → Instagram page setup & visual system
  • → Creative direction for ongoing Instagram content
  • → Promotional flyers & event banners
  • → Event promo video (concept, script, recording, editing)
  • → Instagram Reels
  • → Facebook ad management
  • → Live event production support & DJ

The Event

SK8 Fest Ensenada took place September 30 – October 1, 2023. The organizer expected 250–300 people. About 500 showed up. 100 competitive skaters came from Mexicali, Tijuana, Ensenada, Sonora, Baja California Sur, San Diego, and Los Angeles. The event was tied to local government initiatives around youth sports and community health. SK8 Fest is now an annual event.
