Apartment Complexes

How we handle
large properties.

A full apartment complex repaint is a logistical project, not just a painting job. Here's exactly how we approach it so property managers know what to expect before we start.

→
Full scope walkthrough before quoting

We assess every surface, identify repair needs, and deliver a written proposal with no hidden scope. What we quote is what you pay.

→
Building-by-building phasing

We stage the project so residents always have access to their units and common areas. Work progresses systematically, not chaotically.

→
Resident notification coordination

We work with management to communicate schedules to residents in advance — parking restrictions, access windows, and completion timelines.

→
Progress updates throughout

Property managers get regular updates — no chasing us for status reports. You know where we are every day of the project.

→
Final walkthrough with management

We don't call the job complete until the property manager has walked every building with us and signed off. Touch-ups are handled immediately.

→
Warranty on completed work

We stand behind the finish. If something fails due to our workmanship within a reasonable period, we come back and make it right.
