  • Wood replacement & staining
  • Baseboard installation
  • Surface sanding & skim coating

Our Process

5 steps.
Every single job.

We follow the same disciplined process whether we're painting one room or an entire house. This is what separates a lasting finish from one that needs redoing in two years.

01

Getting the Space Ready

We move furniture, cover everything with plastic sheeting, and protect your floors with drop cloths. Not a single brush stroke before your home is fully protected.

02

Proper Surface Preparation

Every hole filled, every crack sealed, every stain primed, every surface sanded smooth. The prep work is where the quality lives — this step gets the full attention it deserves.

03

The Painting

Proper application techniques and premium Sherwin Williams products deliver a uniform finish that looks sharp now and holds up for years. We don't cut corners on coats or coverage.

04

Thorough Cleanup

Every day and at project completion — we return your furniture, vacuum and sweep, and remove every piece of material and equipment. Your home is left cleaner than we found it.

05

Final Walk-Through Together

We walk every room with you. If there's anything that needs touching up, we handle it on the spot. We don't pack up until you're satisfied with the work.
